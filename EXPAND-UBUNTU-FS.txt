
Expand a newly imaged Raspi filesystem

Ubuntu: 
sudo fdisk /dev/mmcblk0 
  # then enter the following EXACTLY in sequece..
    d   2   n   p   2   enter   enter   n   w   
  reboot
sudo resize2fs /dev/mmcblk0p2

Raspian:
sudo raspi-config
  advanced options 
    expand filesystem
  ok
 finish
yes (to reboot)
